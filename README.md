### 🩷 About Me

I enjoy exploring ideas through both scientific writing and creative expression. Much of my work and thinking exists at the intersection of research and reflection: from studying Seyfert galaxies and space habitats to writing poetry inspired by cosmic metaphors and human emotion.  
I'm learning to think in dual orbits like scientific rigor and huaman connections, peer-reviewed papers and business proposals, mission design and mission economics.  
"The Human Element" is what I always look for

‪‪❤︎‬‪‪ &nbsp;I'm currently working on **Colour, Mind and Space: A systematic review**
